History of Game Station

Game Station was founded on March 21st, 2022 by Alexander Mertsch.

Mertsch was always an avid gamer ever since he was a young boy and has been very passionate about his hobby ever since.

When Mertsch grew up, he got his first job at a retail store, which over time would inspire him to open his own, albiet more specialized.

His vision was quite simple, he wanted his store to feel more than just another video game retailer, he wanted to be a gamer's paraside.

Ever since then, Game Station has been booming throughout Virginia and many other states.


What we're about!

At Game Station, we believe people should be treated how they want to be treated.

As such, we treat our customers as if they are gamers looking for the ultimate experience.

At Game Station, we strive to offer top-of-the-line products and experiences to gamers of all kind.

We not only offer the latest products from the big names, but we also offer our own original products to keep consumers busy.
